Course Overview

These resources are designed to support students in Calculus II, especially as they develop fluency with integration techniques, applications of integration, sequences and series, power series, and Taylor series.

The lecture notes provide a structured written resource for the course, while the video collection gives additional practice with older exam-style problems.

How to Use These Resources

  • Use the blank lecture notes while working through class material, filling in definitions, examples, and explanations as you go.
  • Use the practice exam videos after attempting problems on your own. The videos are most helpful when they become a way to check and refine your reasoning, rather than a substitute for first attempting the work.
  • For exam preparation, focus on recognizing problem types, choosing an appropriate method, and explaining why that method applies.
