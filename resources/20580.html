Course Overview

This page collects digital resources for MATH 20580: Linear Algebra and Differential Equations. These materials include interactive walkthroughs, embedded explanations, audio-supported resources, recorded office hours, and review videos connected to core course topics.

The goal of these resources is not only to help students solve problems, but also to strengthen conceptual understanding, mathematical communication, and long-term retention.

How to Use These Resources

Students often benefit most from these materials when they actively work alongside the examples rather than passively watching or reading.

Try pausing videos before solutions are revealed, working through examples independently, and comparing your reasoning with the walkthroughs afterward. The goal is to develop both computational fluency and conceptual understanding.
