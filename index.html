About

I am an Associate Teaching Professor in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Notre Dame. My work focuses on active learning, student support initiatives, educational technology, and interdisciplinary teaching.

ASCEND

A summer bridge and mentoring program helping students transition into university-level mathematics through coaching, reflection, and community.

AR Calculus III

Augmented reality visualizations for multivariable calculus concepts, helping students interact with 3D mathematical objects.

Survivor and Game Theory

Interdisciplinary teaching exploring decision-making, strategy, neuroscience, and mathematics through the lens of Survivor.

Teaching

Courses include Calculus, Mathematics in Architecture, Linear Algebra & Differential Equations, and interdisciplinary seminars.
